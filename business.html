Business Financing Solutions to help your business grow.

About Business Banking

Address the unique needs of every client

Business Banking segment as per CBE definition is businesses with sales turnover up to EGP 200,000,000 divided into :

Micro Enterprises

-Sales below EGP 1,000,000

-Capital below EGP 50,000

-Number of employees below 10

Small Enterprises

-Sales from EGP 1,000,000 to 50,000,000

-Capital from EGP 50,000 to 5,000,000 for industrial entities & below EGP 3,000,000 for non-industrial entities

-Number of employees below 200

Medium Enterprises

-Sales from EGP 50,000,000 to 200,000,000

-Number of employees below 200

-Capital from EGP 5,000,000 to 15,000,000 for industrial entities & from EGP 3,000,000 to 5,000,000 for non-industrial entities

Cards

Corporate Debit Card

-Issuing Debit Card on company account to be used in all related purchases & payments activities , also earn points on all spending.

Corporate Credit Card

-Issuing a Credit Card with a Credit limit to be used in all related purchases & payments activities then to be settled from company account periodically , also earn points on all spending.

Facilities & loans

Express Financing program for small and medium enterprises

-Fast financing in Egyptian pound, up to 5 million Egyptian pounds

-Simplified procedures and fewer documents required

-Competitive interest rate

-Fixed installments up to 5 years

-Sales from EGP 20 million to EGP 200 million

Small business loan

-Fast financing in Egyptian pound, from 20 thousand up to 1 million Egyptian pounds

-Simplified procedures and fewer documents required

-Fixed installments up to 5 years

-Minimum of 2 Year in business as evidenced by Commercial Register

- Minimum PIC to be EGP 20K

Digital solutions & services

Smart business

-Smart business is an online safe & secured application enabling you to perform all related transactions to your business including (payroll - transfers - ordering check books - issuing manager checks) quickly & easily from your place without the need to visit the bank.

Smart Trade application

-Smart trade application is an online safe & secured application enabling you to perform all related trade transactions to your business like (issuing letters of guarantees, issuing letter of credit, incoming documents for collection) quickly & easily from your place without the need to visit the bank.

Corporate Payment Service (CPS)

-Digital & safe solution interface in cooperation with E-finance to pay all your governmental payments right from your office.

Non Life Insurance

-All insurance products related to your type of business to insure safe and secure transactions.
